#Plants with this trait can be bred to automatically harvest itself.
<b><color=\#09e530>Stacks</color></b>
<b><color=\#1ab7d5>Default</color></b> - Plant's grow days / 2
<b><color=\#6c8be4>Notice</color></b> - When upgrading: the upgrade will check whether the multplier or default is less and will select the smaller value
<b><color=\#6c8be4>Notice</color></b> - Plants will sow over wild plants but not player sowed plants nor trees
