#Plants with this trait can be bred to automatically harvest itself.
<b><color=\#09e530>Stacks</color></b> - Limit to 5 levels
<b><color=\#1ab7d5>Default</color></b> - 65%
<b><color=\#6c8be4>Notice</color></b> - Plant will not be automatically harvested by pawns,
<b><color=\#6c8be4>Notice</color></b> - Harvest multiplier cannot exceed 100%, 
<b><color=\#6c8be4>Notice</color></b> - Plant will always harvest at least 1 produce
